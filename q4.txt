Improve Web Accessibility :--
1:-Size and Space for approach and Use: Appropriate size and space is provided for approach,
 reach, manipulation, and use regardless of user's body size, posture, or mobility.

 2:-Equitable Use: The design is useful and marketable to people with diverse abilities.

 3:-Perceptible Information: The design communicates necessary information effectively to 
 the user, regardless of ambient conditions or the user's sensory abilities.
